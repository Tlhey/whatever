4-1

1 Pressure
def:
the force exerted per unit area by particles as they strike the surface around them.
formula
Pressure = force / area (P = F/A)
unit
1 Pa = 1 N/m²
1 atm = 101325 N/m²
others
0K = 273.15 °C

2 Molar Volume
def
volume occupied by one mole at STP
STP:
standard temperature (273 K, 0 °C) and pressure (1.00 atm) (STP)
3 Density of Gases
def
Density = M/V = PM/RT

4-2

1 Dalton’s law of partial pressures
cond: non-reacting gases
Ptotal = Pa + Pb + Pc + …

2 Mole fraction
Xa = na / ntotal

3 Kinetic Theory
cond:
1. particle is negligibly small
2. average kinetic energy ∝ K
3. completely elastic collision
Average Kinetic Energy Formula:
K = 3/2 * R * T / NA

4 Real Gas
Conclu:
在标准状况 (STP) 下，理想气体模型仍然是一个很好的近似。
Finite Volume
larger volume
1 Nonideal behavior
2 pressure influence
Corrected for Finite Volume:
V = nRT / P + nb
Intermolecular Force
smaller pressure
The attraction force enhances the interactions among particles but reduces the collisions with wall surfaces, resulting in lower pressure
这种吸引力会稍微“拉回”粒子，使其撞击器壁的力和频率减小
Temperature
在高温下，粒子运动速度很快，分子间作用力的影响变得相对不那么显著。
温度越高, 越理想
Corrected for intermolecular forces
P = nRT / V - a * (n/V)^2
Van der Waals Equation
[P + a(n/V)²] × [V - nb] = nRT

4-3

1 Energy
classificataion:
kinetic: thermoal (T)
potential: position of e and nuclei composition(化学键)
unit
kg * (m/s)² 1 KE = ½ mv²
1 Joule (J) is defined as 1 kg * m²/s².
1 calorie (cal) = 4.184 joules (J)
1 kilowatt-hour (kWh) = 3.60 × 10⁶ J

2 The First Law of Thermodynamics
The total energy of the universe is constant.

3 Internal Energy
def E
sum of the kinetic and potential energies
def E func
The internal energy is a state function. 状态函数是一个系统的性质，只取决于系统的当前状态，而与达到该状态的路径无关
ΔE
def
internal energy change between the final and initial states
for
ΔE = E_final - E_initial
example
ΔE > 0 (正值)，表示系统的内能增加了，通常是系统吸收了能量
ΔE < 0 (负值)，表示系统的内能减少了，通常是系统释放了能量

Energy Flow     
    ΔE = q + w      
    exothermic: absorbs heat
        ex:
            CO2 -> c + o2
    endothermic: releases heat 
        ex 
            c + o2 -> co2

4 Enthalpy (H)
def
the sum of its internal energy and the product of its pressure and volume
for
H = E + PV
det H = det E + P det V
diff:
∆E measures all of the energy (heat and work)
∆H only measures the heat exchange under conditions of constant pressure
notes:
If a chemical reaction does not cause a large change in reaction volume as they occur, ∆H and ∆e are almost the same in value.

5 Standard Enthalpy Change
Hess’s Law: 反应可叠加
det H
Standard Enthalpy Change
Standard State
gas: 1 atm
soild/liquid 1 atm 25C
solution: 1 mol
ΔH°
当所有反应物和生成物都在标准态时，化学反应的焓变
ΔHf°
1 mol 化合物由标准态的单质生成时的焓变, 单质在标准态：它的 ΔHf° = 0。

rxn formula
    ΔH° = sum n(ΔHf°(products)) - sum n(ΔHf°(reactants))

1 Energy Spreads Out (能量扩散)
Energy spreads out or disperses from a more concentrated to a less concentrated form.
2 Spontaneous Process (自发过程)
def
spon: occurs without ongoing outside intervention
not necessay fast
non-spon: NOT occur without ongoing (持续地提供能量或进行干预) outside intervention
nonspontaneous process can happen
ex:
Spontaneous Mixing

3 Entropy (S)
def
a thermodynamic function of the degree of disorder or randomness
混乱度或无序度

Boltzmann Formula
    S = k ln W  unit: J/K
        k = 1.381 × 10^-23 J/K Boltzmann constant 
        W = the number of microstates
    W
        microstate: rotatino/ translation/ vibration
ΔS 
    ΔS = Sfinal - Sinitial (state function)

4 The Second Law of Thermodynamics
def
For any spontaneous process, the entropy of the universe increases (ΔSuniv > 0)
for
ΔSuniv = ΔSsys + ΔSsurr

5 examples of spon
Water melting
Orderly molecules → Somewhat disorderly molecules
Water evaporating
Somewhat disorderly molecules → Highly disorderly molecules
NaCl dissolving
Orderly ions in crystal lattice→ Disorderly ions
规律
Ssolid < Sliquid < Sgas
An increase in the number of moles of a gas during a chemical reaction.

6 Gibbs Free Energy
def
G = H - TS

    change:
        ΔG = ΔH - TΔS
        ΔG：吉布斯自由能变。
        ΔH：焓变。
        ΔS：熵变。
spon
    ΔG < 0 : spontaneous
    ΔG > 0 : non
    ΔG = 0 : equilibrium
